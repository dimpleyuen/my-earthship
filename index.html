Tip: Composting

Tip: Power Saving Mode

Win-Win: Utilizing the power saving mode on your laptop not only saves energy, but extends your battery life!

Tip: Stop Junk Mail

Taking 10 minutes to opt-out saves an average of 69lbs of junk mail sent to your home each year!

Tip: Plastics Not Fantastic

If we all gave up bottled water, we can minimize up to 154 tonnes (that's almost 7 double decker buses) of waste every day!

Tip: Vampire Power

Eliminating vampire power (aka electricity that continues to run through your appliances when turned off) can save as much as 11% of our electricity usage.

Tip: DIY Decorations

Why purchase outrageously expensive decorations when you can make your own? Find inspiration on Pinterest today! :)

Tip: Choose Local Organic Food

Most of our food are imported +1000 miles away. Imagine all the chemicals used to keep that one tomato red and plump all those days?

Tip: Using Natural Cleaners

Research on some home-made options: not only do they work wonders, they are much safer for family and pets and can save tonnes of packaging too!

Tip: Borrow Or Buy E-Books

Did you know borrowing books from the library is FREE? Furthermore, if you're not fussed about flipping real pages, e-books are the way to go.
